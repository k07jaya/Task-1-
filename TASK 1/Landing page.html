Jaipur

Hawa mahal

Built from red and pink sandstone, it is on the edge of the City Palace, Jaipur.The structure was built in 1799 by the Maharaja Sawai Pratap Singh.This palace is a five-story pyramidal shaped monument that rises to about 50 feet (15 m).

Udaipur

Lake palace

Lake Palace (formally known as Jag Niwas) is a former summer palace of the royal dynasty of Mewar, it is now turned into a hotel. The Lake Palace is located on the island of Jag Niwas in Lake Pichola, Udaipur.

Jodhpur

Mandore Gardens

The garden is a part of the more massive Mehrangarh Fort, and also houses the famous Hall of Heroes and the temple of Three Hundred Million Gods. The garden also has a Government Museum, which is full of artefacts and old relics.

Jaisalmer

Jaisalmer fort

Jaisalmer fort was built in 1156 by King Rawal Jaisal ,near the border of India and Pakistan. The magnificent complex, also known as Sonar Kila ("Golden Fort") for its gleaming golden sandstone walls and buildings, flourished on the east-west caravan route connecting India and Central Asia to the Middle East and North Africa.
